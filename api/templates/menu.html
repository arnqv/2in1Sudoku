Sudoku

Instructions

9×9 square must be filled in with numbers from 1-9 with no repeated numbers in each line, horizontally or vertically. To challenge you more, there are 3×3 squares marked out in the grid, and each of these squares can't have any repeat numbers either

Features
  • Click on "Play!" to play a Sudoku of a certain difficulty (easy, medium, hard, or insane)
  • Click on "Solve Your Own!" to input your own Sudoku
  • Change Theme: Choose between dark, tan, light, and retro themes
