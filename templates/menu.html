Sudoku Menu

How To Play Sudoku

The grid is divided into nine blocks, each containing nine squares. Each of the nine blocks has to contain all the numbers 1-9 within its squares. Each number can only appear once in a row, column, or block.

Features
  • Click on "Play a Sudoku" to either play a Sudoku of a certain difficulty (easy, medium, hard, or expert) or input your own Sudoku
  • Click on "Solve a Sudoku" to enter the Sudoku Solver, which allows you to input any Sudoku and see the finished solution
  • Change Theme: Choose between dark, tan, light, and retro themes
